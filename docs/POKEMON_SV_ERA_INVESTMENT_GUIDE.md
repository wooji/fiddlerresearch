## SECTION 4: FORMAT COMPARISON (ETB vs Booster Box vs UPC)

### Appreciation Curves by Format

**Elite Trainer Box (ETB) — WINNER for long-hold (12–18 month peak)**
- **Appreciation range:** 2–10× over MSRP (set-dependent)
- **Current examples:**
  - Obsidian Flames ETB: $26.77 MSRP → $292 (+991% in 34 months)
  - Ascended Heroes ETB: $59.99 MSRP → $176 (+194% in 5 months, tracking high)
  - Prismatic Evolutions ETB: $70 MSRP → $150–176 (+114–143% in 17 months)
- **Peak timing:** 12–18 months post-release (slow climb, sustained plateau)
- **Why:** Fixed print run per set, retail scarcity builds slowly, sealed collector hoard dynamics = appreciation accelerates month 6–18

**Booster Box (BB) — Strong hold, underperforms ETB**
- **Appreciation range:** 1.8–3.3× over MSRP
- **Current examples:**
  - Obsidian Flames BB: $97 MSRP → $330 (+306% in 34 months, ATH $529 = +543%)
  - Paradox Rift BB: $87 MSRP → $267 (+206% in 19 months, ATH $500)
  - Destined Rivals BB: ~$254 MSRP → $380 (+50% in 13 months, ATH $629 = +148%)
- **Peak timing:** 9–14 months post-release (faster than ETB, shorter plateau)
- **Why:** Higher print volume than ETB, but wholesale scarcity still develops. Tournament demand (players buy for playsets) maintains floor.

**Booster Bundle (Retail Multi-pack) — Flip-only (3–4 weeks maximum hold)**
- **Appreciation range:** 1.5–2× in flip window only; decay after month 3
- **Flip strategy:** Buy at MSRP week 1, sell presale hype week 2–3 for +40–80% ROI, then exit
- **Exception:** Crown Zenith Bundle (+402% over 23 months) = outlier where entire product supply dried up. Not repeatable.
- **Why:** Perpetual retail restock, high print volumes, weakest scarcity floor. Hold >4 weeks and you're competing against ongoing retail supply.

**Ultra Premium Collection (UPC) — Limited data, 2.5–4× estimated (6–12 month hold)**
- **Supply:** Exclusive releases only (not every set), $79.99–$99.99 MSRP
- **Peak window:** 6–12 months post-release (faster than ETB, similar to BB)
- **Flip potential:** Presale week 1–2 only (+30–50%)
- **Hold potential:** 6–12 month hold for 2–4× (between BB and ETB)
- **Gap:** PriceCharting doesn't track UPC separately; data inferred from eBay/TCGPlayer live asks. Use TCGPlayer market price as proxy.

**ETB:** Buy at retail 2–4 weeks post-release (when hype dies), hold 12–18 months for 2–10×  
**BB:** Similar pattern, but peak is month 9–14, and multiples are 30–40% lower than ETB  
**Bundle:** Flip within 3 weeks post-release only; avoid long holds (decay to retail baseline)  
**UPC:** Flip presale week 1–2 for +30–50%, or hold 6–12 months for 2–4× (depends on IP)

- **Probability:** 40–75% for standard flagship sets (SV1, SV2, SV5, SV6, SV7) within 2 years
- **Announcement window:** 2–12 months post-release (earlier = stronger demand signal, later = lower risk)
- **Price impact:** 20–90% dip on reprint announcement (Twilight Masquerade -90%, Paldean Fates -55%)
- **Recovery timeline:** 6–24 months post-floor (IP-dependent; strong IP rebounds fast, weak IP stagnates)
- **Mitigation:** Avoid buying flagship sets >6 months post-release (past peak demand, reprint risk rising). Buy limited/mythical sets only (zero reprint probability).

### Mechanic Fatigue

- **Pattern:** Gimmick mechanics (Paradox, Mythical) peak month 2–3, then bleed 3–5% per month as novelty wears off
- **Duration:** 6–12 months to complete bleed cycle
- **Recovery:** Weak (only if IP is S-tier, e.g., Paradox Rift sustained by Koraidon legend demand)

### Competitive Format Rotation

- **Cycle:** Pokemon rotates standard format every April (ex: H-mark sets legal through 2026–27, I-mark starting 2026, J-mark 2027)
- **Impact:** Older format sets appreciate into rotation (final season = peak demand), then depreciate post-rotation (collectors move to new legal set)
- **Timing:** Buy 3–4 months before format rotation (final season approval), sell 2–3 months after (when new rotation takes effect)
- **Mitigation:** Monitor Pokemon TCG format announcements (Pokemon.com, championship schedules) for rotation dates.

- **Impact:** SV-era sealed likely reprinted aggressively in 2027–2028 window before new facility operations
- **Mitigation:** Sell high-print-run sets (SV1, SV7, SV9) by EOY 2027 (before reprint wave). Hold limited/mythical sets indefinitely (permanent scarcity).
