## ETB Appreciation Mechanics (The Investment Standard)

### Trajectory Pattern (S/A-Tier IP)
```
Months 0–6:    HOLD PHASE
├─ Retail supply abundant at MSRP + 5–15%
├─ Presale ask premium: +20–60% above MSRP
└─ Month 6 settlement: $35–50 (presale excess shakes out)

Months 6–12:   FLAT PHASE
├─ Secondary supply tightens
├─ Presale buyers take profit
├─ Market stabilizes $40–80 range
└─ Limited appreciation (flat to +10%)

Months 12–18:  ACCELERATION PHASE ⭐ (Peak Entry Window)
├─ Secondary supply becomes scarce
├─ Collector retention rises (long-hold buyers emerge)
├─ Month 12–14: sharp climb begins
├─ Month 14–18: exponential climb (fastest ROI growth)
└─ ETB appreciation *typically* exceeds BB by 30–40% in this window

Months 18–36+: SUSTAINED CLIMB PHASE
├─ Peak window extends (unlike booster box)
├─ Demand remains strong (sealed scarcity, collectibility)
├─ Appreciation curve flattens *slower* than BB
└─ Multi-year holds viable for S-tier IP (Charizard, Eevee, Pikachu)
```

## Key Takeaways (Investment Thesis)

1. **ETB is the exclusive investment format** — 30–40% higher appreciation than booster box for same IP over 12 months. Peak window extends month 14–18+ (longer than BB's month 9–14).

2. **IP strength drives appreciation, not format** — S-tier IP (Charizard, Eevee) = 2.5–10× multiples; C-tier IP (Pecharunt) = below MSRP regardless of format. Format determines speed and peak window, not whether appreciation occurs.

3. **Presale velocity ≥+40% = scarcity confirmed** — Use presale premium as a gate: +40%+ presale = hold 12–18 months. <+10% presale = flip-only (week 2–3).

4. **2026 sets are supply-gated from day 1** — Fixed pre-print runs (H/I/J marks) eliminate point-of-demand reprints. All 2026 releases have a structural appreciation floor.

5. **Month 12–14 is the critical entry window for ETBs** — Flat phase ends, exponential climb begins. Buying at month 12–13 (not presale) often locks in better entry than presale asks and captures the steep curve.

6. **Millennium Print Group risk: sell mass-print by EOY 2027** — Hold event-limited and 30th Celebration through 2028+ (format/event scarcity protects). Mass-print flagships (standard Charizard, Pikachu) must exit by Sep 2027.

7. **eBay SOLD median is the only authoritative price** — TCGPlayer live asks are +15–30% inflated. PriceCharting averages lag by 10–20%. Presale asks are +20–80% inflated (FOMO). Always validate appreciation against eBay sold comps.

8. **UPC lacks historical data; validate post-release only** — Never use presale asks for UPC ROI. Pull eBay SOLD comps (past 30 days) after release day 1, compare to MSRP, and gate appreciation on real transactions.

9. **30th Celebration (Sep 2026) is unprecedented** — All-foil 6-card format, Futuristic Rare debut, format locked (no reprint). Expect UPC-tier appreciation (3–4× month 12). Watch presale premium as scarcity signal.

10. **Reprint announcement window = month 3–8** — If no reprint announced by month 8, set is protected through month 36+. Reprints drop presale -50% but only flagship IP recover (+5× if Charizard/Eevee; no recovery if mythical-only).
