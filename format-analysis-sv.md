## Format-Specific Mechanics

### Elite Trainer Box (ETB)

**Supply Characteristics:**
- **Fixed print run** per set (sealed case production caps restock)
- **Retail scarcity** → collectors/investors hoard for sealed appreciation
- **Pokemon Center + retail distribution** (Target, Walmart, card shops) → supply bottleneck post-release
- **Opened singles market** decays; sealed ETB holds/appreciates

**Appreciation Pattern (SV Era):**
- Month 0–5: Flat or slight decline (post-hype, retail still stocked)
- Month 6–10: Acceleration begins as retail dries up
- Month 12+: Steep climb (2–10x depending on IP strength)
- **Peak Window:** 12–18 months post-release

**Flip vs Hold:**
- NOT a short flip format (peaks too slow)
- **Best strategy:** Buy at retail MSRP 2–4 weeks post-release (when hype dies), hold 12+ months
- Expected ROI: **+100–900%** over 12–18 months depending on set IP

**Sets Validating Pattern:**
- Obsidian Flames: +991% at 34 months
- Ascended Heroes: +194% at 5 months (still climbing)
- Prismatic Evolutions: +114–143% at 17 months (still climbing)

---

### Booster Box (BB)

**Supply Characteristics:**
- **Higher print volumes** than ETB (core product)
- **Distributor/LGS channels** → wholesale availability persists longer
- **Sealed case scarcity** develops, but NOT as severe as ETB
- **Tournament play + league supply** creates baseline demand (not pure speculation)

**Appreciation Pattern (SV Era):**
- Month 0–8: Floor hold (wholesale supply + casual demand balance)
- Month 9–14: Acceleration (supply tightens)
- Month 15+: Sustained appreciation but lower slope than ETB

**Flip vs Hold:**
- **Limited flip window** (2–4 weeks post-release, pre-equilibrium)
- **Better hold candidate** than Bundle; worse than ETB for long-term ROI
- Expected ROI: **+100–250%** over 12–18 months

**Sets Validating Pattern:**
- Obsidian Flames: +306% (currently), ATH +543%
- Paradox Rift: +234% (currently), ATH +475%
- Ascended Heroes: ~+100% (early, 5 months in)

### Booster Bundle (Retail Packs)

**Supply Characteristics:**
- **Perpetual restock** at retail (lowest-margin product)
- **High print volumes** → scalper supply on Amazon/eBay
- **Weakest scarcity floor** (retailers restock bundles aggressively)
- **Target/Walmart exclusive SKUs** → easier for investors to arbitrage away

**Appreciation Pattern (SV Era):**
- Month 0–2: Flip window (1.5–2x presale ask to MSRP arbitrage)
- Month 3–6: Decay back toward MSRP or slight premium
- Month 7+: Slow climb IF extreme scarcity (rare for bundles)
- **Peak Window:** 2–8 weeks post-release (not a long hold)

**Flip vs Hold:**
- **Flip-only format**. Should NOT be held 6+ months.
- Expected ROI: **+40–80%** in 2–4 week window; below that, decay
- Sets showing sustained appreciation are exceptions (Crown Zenith +402% is abnormal; usually because entire set supply dried up)

**Sets Validating Pattern:**
- Obsidian Flames Bundle: +500% (extreme outlier; entire set supply scarce)
- Crown Zenith Bundle: +402% (took 8+ months; not a flip)
- Paradox Rift Bundle: +195% (2-year hold, now peaking)

## Critical Caveats

### UPC Data Limitation
- No historical PriceCharting data for UPC (premium format)
- Market pricing inferred from **live eBay/TCGPlayer asks**, not sold comps
- Presale prices are **ask-inflated**; real sold comps appear after release day 1
- **Always validate UPC appreciation against eBay *sold* listings, not active asks**

### Supply Unpredictability
- Pokemon print runs are not public; supply constraints are inferred from price action
- Millennium Print Group going live ~2028 = **long-term supply shock** for current sealed stock
- Reprints can crater multiples (e.g., Crown Zenith reprinted in 2024 = price reset risk)

### IP Strength Variance
- Sets with weak anchor characters (Zygarde, Dragalge, Floette) plateau early
- Evergreen IP (Eevee, Charizard, Mewtwo) sustain 2–3x+ multiples past 18 months
- Darkrai (Pitch Black anchor) has moderate strength (80/100 index) — expect 2–3x, not 5–10x

### Presale vs. Real Market
- Presale TCGPlayer/eBay asks are NOT equivalent to sold comps
- Always wait for post-release eBay sold data before projecting multiples
- Presale hype often inflates asks 20–30%; real market settles 10–15% lower
