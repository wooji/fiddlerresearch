## Key Takeaways

1. **Lorcana appreciation is IP-driven.** Buy only if character has nostalgic weight (Maleficent, Ursula, Frozen). Skip Jafar, Rapunzel, ensemble casts.

2. **Hold window is 6–12 months.** Sets older than 18 months face EOL discounting. Don't hold into the cliff.

3. **Reprints destroy value.** Monitor Ravensburger announcements monthly. Exit immediately on reprint news.

4. **Booster box only.** All other formats (Troves, gift sets, packs) trade at retail. Zero appreciation potential.

5. **Exit on 50–100% ROI.** Lock gains early. Don't wait for maximum ATH; sell at 1.5–2× retail and redeploy to new releases.

6. **Compare to Pokemon, not against it.** Lorcana is a niche IP-appreciation play, not a liquidity vehicle. Smaller market, higher volatility, but comparable short-term ROI.
