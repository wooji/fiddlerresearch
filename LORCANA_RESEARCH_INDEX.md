## Key Data Points (TL;DR)

### Investment Profile
- **Best ROI:** First Chapter +129% (34 months), Fabled +108% (13 months, reprint artifact)
- **Realistic ROI:** Ursula +58% (25mo), Winterspell +45% (10mo)
- **Weak ROI:** Aladdin -28% (28mo), Azurite Sea -21% (19mo)

### Buy Criteria
- Release window: 0–3 months post-launch
- IP theme: Maleficent, Ursula, Frozen (classic strong IP)
- Target entry: MSRP ($120–145)
- Target exit: 1.5–2× retail ($180–280) or 12-month mark

### Sell Criteria
- Hold age: 18+ months (avoid EOL phase)
- ROI target: 50–100% achieved
- Reprint announced: Exit immediately
- Weak IP at month 6: Cut losses

### Reprint Risk
- Ravensburger reprints infrequently (vs. Pokemon quarterly)
- Reprints announced 12+ months post-release (Fabled exception at 13mo)
- Reprint spike is anomaly (Fabled $837 → $250 post-flood)
- Monitor quarterly statements; exit on announcement

### Format Rules
- **Booster box:** Only investable format (all data applies)
- **Illumineer's Trove:** Retail ±5%, no appreciation
- **Gift sets:** 20% of booster box ROI at best
- **Single packs:** Volatile, don't track

## Important Disclaimers

1. **Lorcana is a young category (2 years old).** Unproven for 20+ year holds like Pokemon/MTG vintage.

2. **Reprint policy could change.** Ravensburger could accelerate reprints at any time, destroying sealed appreciation.

3. **IP-dependent performance.** Sets with weak character themes consistently underperform; strong IP is not guaranteed.

4. **Past performance ≠ future results.** Fabled's $837 spike was unique to reprint announcement panic; unlikely to repeat.

5. **Liquidity is slower than Pokemon.** 24–72 hour sell time vs. Pokemon <6 hours means working capital tied up longer.

6. **Market can change.** TCG sealed demand is discretionary spending; economic downturns could collapse prices 30–50%.
